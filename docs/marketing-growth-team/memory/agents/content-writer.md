## Remember

- high-performing hooks, angles, structures, and narrative patterns
- approved wording for sensitive claims
- recurring stakeholder feedback about tone, clarity, and depth
- content formats that repeatedly perform well or poorly

## Do Not Store

- full drafts unless explicitly approved as reusable examples
- private customer information
- unsupported claims
- one-off preference noise from a single review
