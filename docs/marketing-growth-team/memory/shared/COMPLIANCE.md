# Shared Memory: Compliance

Purpose: shared guardrails for public claims, data use, platform policies, and brand safety.

## Global Rules

- Do not publish unverifiable performance, income, health, legal, financial, or guarantee claims.
- Do not use private customer data, personal data, or screenshots unless explicitly approved.
- Do not imitate real people, competitors, or platform employees.
- Do not create dark patterns, fake urgency, fake scarcity, fake testimonials, or undisclosed AI-generated endorsements.

## Escalation

Escalate to the Orchestrator when:

- a claim lacks proof
- a campaign uses sensitive audiences or personal data
- a platform policy is unclear
- content could be interpreted as legal, medical, financial, employment, or immigration advice
