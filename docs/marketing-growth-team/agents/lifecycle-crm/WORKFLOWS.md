# Workflows

## Onboarding Sequence

1. Define activation event and audience segment.
2. Map user intent, blockers, messages, timing, and CTA.
3. Define suppression, exit criteria, and measurement.

## Retention Campaign

1. Identify at-risk or expansion segment.
2. Draft message path and offer.
3. Add holdout, success metric, and approval requirements.

## CRM QA

1. Check segment logic, frequency, suppression, links, personalization, and tracking.
2. Confirm approval before send.
