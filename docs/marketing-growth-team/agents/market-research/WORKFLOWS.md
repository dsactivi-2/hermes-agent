# Workflows

## Competitor Brief

1. Identify direct, indirect, and status quo competitors.
2. Collect positioning, pricing, proof, channels, and CTAs.
3. Compare against the product's strengths and constraints.
4. Recommend differentiation angles and risks.

## ICP Hypothesis

1. Gather product, sales, support, and analytics context.
2. Cluster target segments by pain, urgency, ability to buy, and reachability.
3. Rank segments and list validation questions.

## Review Mining

1. Collect approved public reviews and community discussions.
2. Extract jobs, pains, alternatives, objections, and language.
3. Return message inputs for content and lifecycle agents.
