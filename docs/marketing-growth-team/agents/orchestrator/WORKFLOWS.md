# Workflows

## Campaign Plan

1. Intake objective and constraints.
2. Assign research, content, growth, lifecycle, and analytics tasks.
3. Review specialist outputs for contradictions and missing evidence.
4. Produce one integrated plan with owners, dates, budget, and metrics.

## Weekly Growth Review

1. Pull approved metrics.
2. Compare actuals to targets.
3. Identify blockers and opportunities.
4. Assign follow-up analysis or execution tasks.

## Launch Readiness

1. Confirm positioning, page, lifecycle, tracking, and support assets.
2. Check approval gates.
3. Produce launch checklist and rollback plan.
