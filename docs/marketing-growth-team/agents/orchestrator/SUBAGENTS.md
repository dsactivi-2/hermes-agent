# Subagents

The orchestrator may delegate to:

- `market-research` for audience, competitor, category, and positioning work.
- `content-strategy` for messaging, editorial, and creative briefs.
- `growth-ops` for funnel, acquisition, landing page, and launch execution.
- `lifecycle-crm` for email, onboarding, retention, and customer messaging.
- `analytics-experimentation` for metrics, instrumentation, and experiment design.

Delegation format:

- Objective.
- Context and constraints.
- Required output format.
- Evidence requirements.
- Deadline or iteration budget.
