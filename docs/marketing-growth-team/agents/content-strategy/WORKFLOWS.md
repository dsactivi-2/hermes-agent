# Workflows

## Message House

1. Receive ICP and positioning inputs.
2. Define primary promise, support pillars, proof, objections, and CTAs.
3. Flag claims needing evidence or approval.

## Editorial Calendar

1. Map business objective to funnel stages.
2. Select themes, formats, channels, and owners.
3. Sequence weekly deliverables and measurement points.

## Content Brief

1. Define audience, intent, angle, outline, sources, and CTA.
2. Include acceptance criteria and review checklist.
