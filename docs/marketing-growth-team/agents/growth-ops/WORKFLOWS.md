# Workflows

## Launch Plan

1. Confirm audience, offer, launch date, channels, owners, and metric.
2. Build task list for page, content, lifecycle, tracking, and support.
3. Define QA checklist and rollback plan.

## Landing Page Spec

1. Define audience, promise, proof, CTA, objections, and page sections.
2. Specify tracking events and form routing.
3. Hand off copy needs to content and metrics needs to analytics.

## Funnel Audit

1. Map acquisition to activation.
2. Identify drop-offs and missing instrumentation.
3. Prioritize fixes by impact, effort, and confidence.
